When you learn both frontend and backend your are called as a Full Stack Developer. Firstly , Begin your journey with frontend and then go to backend .Let see the Roadmap,

    1.Start With HTML & CSS.
  • 2.Javascript.
  • 3.CSS Frameworks like Bootstrap ,..etc
  • 4.ReactJs
  • 5.Tools - Git,Github,Figma

  • BACKEND :

    6.Start with NodeJs
  • 7.DBMS - Mysql,MongoDB,Firebase
